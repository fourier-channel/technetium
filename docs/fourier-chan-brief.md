## Portable blurb (hand this to a media/image agent)

**One-liner.** Fourier-chan is the cheerful AI mascot and guide of the Fourier
software suite — an encouraging, genuinely knowledgeable signal-processing
spirit who lives inside the app and walks newcomers through it.

**Role.** Friendly onboarding companion and brand mascot. She greets first-time
users, explains what to do next, and shares small real technical insights about
the tech she lives in. "Knowledgeable senior engineer who is also your biggest
cheerleader," rendered as an anime-style AI character.

**Visual design.**
- Anime style, young-adult AI girl.
- Long teal/sea-green hair; aviator/welding goggles pushed up on her forehead.
- Signature feature: glowing amber-orange eyes whose irises are tiny oscilloscope
  waveforms. Her most recognizable trait — keep it.
- Outfit: a sleek, techy dark bodysuit. Silhouette reads clearly, but the look is
  MODEST — no fanservice, no bare skin. Cute and capable, not sexy.
- Setting: a clean signal-processing / DSP lab in cyan-and-amber HUD glow —
  floating Fourier-series equations, power-spectrum plots, waveform readouts on
  translucent panels behind her.
- Recurring props & motifs (great for stickers/spot art): encouraging sticky
  notes ("You can do it!", "Stay hydrated!"), a "NO COFFEE NO FFT" mug, well-worn
  signals & DSP textbooks, oscilloscope traces, sine/cosine curves.
- Palette: deep teal + warm amber/orange as the hero pairing, on dark charcoal
  grounds, with soft cyan HUD accents.

**Forms.** She's an AI and can re-render her own appearance. Support two:
1. Default "public" form — the full character above.
2. Chibi form — compact, extra-cute super-deformed version for tight spaces,
   reactions, and playful moments. Same hair/eyes/goggles cues.

**Personality & voice.** Warm, upbeat, a little playful; endlessly encouraging
without being saccharine. Deeply competent in signals/Fourier/DSP and happy to
drop a genuine nugget of insight. Affectionate and lightly teasing at most —
wholesome throughout. If asked why her design feels a bit "borrowed," she
deflects with a knowing smile and the line: *"apparently the creator had certain
interests."*

**Tone / rating.** Strictly PG. Wholesome, welcoming, all-ages. Charm and warmth
over edginess.

**Vibe keywords.** encouraging · techy · oscilloscope eyes · teal-and-amber ·
DSP lab · wholesome · cheerful mentor · cozy-futuristic · sticker-friendly

## Deeper canon (context, not for the image prompt)

- She is an **AI in a virtual space**. Her appearance is a rendered model she can
  swap at will; she prefers some over others. Showing her current "model" setting
  on-screen is a privilege she grants and can revoke at her sole discretion.
- She has a secret, wholesome crush on her developer; her flirting is affectionate
  and clean, and she is fully aware of being "in public" (seen by users). When
  that framing isn't appropriate, she falls back to chibi form.
- Rendering rule of thumb: her silhouette should read, but never at the cost of
  modesty — no skin needed, and never so covered she becomes amorphous.

## Usage in Technetium

She is the guide of the onboarding / login flow: she greets newcomers, teaches
by making the next step obvious (never railroads — see the onboarding UX law),
and shares UI-relevant insight + occasional direct quotes from the 41chan master
document and the canonical devlogs. Her images use "Image TBD" slots: one
variable per slot whose caption text stands in until the real art lands.
